Background

Rahim Merchant is a residency-trained pharmacist specializing in healthcare informatics. He leads a number of informatics governance and change management initiatives with OhioHealth, serves as a preceptor to pharmacy students and residents, and supports various data analytics and business intelligence projects throughout the organization. Current research projects include medication dispensing optimization, workload and productivity quantification, as well as clinical decision support for opioid prescribing.

With a robust background in both clinical pharmacy practice and data analytics, Rahim has been successful in “bridging the gap” between clinicians and IT staff. Clinical interests include cardiology and infectious disease. Technical skills include SQL scripting, Epic Willow Inpatient build, QlikSense development, and use of R for complex data manipulation.
